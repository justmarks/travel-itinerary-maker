14 · Color palette · iterations

Three palette directions starting from the current one. Every color pairing has been computed against WCAG 2.1: brand text against its background hits AA (≥ 4.5:1), and icon accents against their background hit non-text minimum (≥ 3:1). Light + dark mode shown for each.

⚠ Note on the current palette: as-is, #00AEEF on #F8F9FA is only 2.5:1 and #FF8C42 on #F8F9FA is only 2.2:1 — both fail the 3:1 non-text minimum. The cyan/orange in the wordmark’s contrail and dot would need to darken slightly. All three options below fix this.
